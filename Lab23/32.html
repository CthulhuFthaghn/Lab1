Night City

Unbeknownst to V, his insatiable appetite for wealth draws the attention of the powerful corporation he has been targeting.
As V's schemes escalate, so does the corporation's resolve to stop them. In a dramatic showdown, corporate enforcers track down V's hideout, his greed finally leading to their downfall.
Surrounded and outnumbered, V's last stand ends in a hail of gunfire, his dreams of wealth shattered by the very greed that consumed him.
As the neon lights flicker and fade, V's demise serves as a grim reminder of the consequences of unchecked ambition in a world where power and greed reign supreme.
