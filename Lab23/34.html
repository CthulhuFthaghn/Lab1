Night City

As V maneuvers through the cybernetic metropolis, they entertain the idea of joining Arasaka for personal gain.
Discovering Arasaka's corruption, V sees an opportunity to leverage their skills for power and profit.
However, the path to joining Arasaka is rife with moral ambiguity, and V must weigh the consequences of aligning with a corrupt corporation.
In the end, V's pursuit of personal interest shapes their destiny and influences the city's future in a world where ethics and ambition collide.
